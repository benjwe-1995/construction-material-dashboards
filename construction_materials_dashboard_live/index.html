Construction Materials Proxy Price Dashboard

Tracks monthly price-index proxies for selected construction materials and equipment. These are not Singapore supplier quotes or spot prices; they are public Producer Price Index series, mainly from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics via FRED.
Interpretation note: AHUs, chillers, lifts, transformers and gen sets are usually project-specific equipment, so there is rarely a clean market price. Use these series as escalation indicators only. Each chart states its exact proxy and source.
